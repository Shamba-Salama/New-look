About SalamaFarm

SalamaFarm is an AI-powered smart scarecrow designed to effectively deter pests during critical harvesting times.

Our intelligent system adapts to your farm's needs, ensuring maximum crop protection with minimal effort. No matter your experience level, we'll guide you every step of the way!

Specifically designed for rice farms, SalamaFarm uses advanced technology to detect and deter birds without harming them or the environment. Our solution combines cutting-edge AI with practical farm knowledge to create the most effective bird deterrent system available.

Our Innovation

Traditional scarecrows have remained largely unchanged for centuries, proving ineffective as birds quickly adapt to static deterrents. SalamaFarm revolutionizes this ancient concept with modern technology.

To create a world where farmers can protect their crops effectively without harming wildlife or the environment, using intelligent, adaptive technology that works in harmony with nature.

To develop and deploy affordable, effective AI-powered agricultural solutions that empower farmers to maximize their yields while minimizing environmental impact.

Innovation, Sustainability, Farmer-Centric Design, Ethical Technology, and Continuous Improvement drive everything we do at SalamaFarm.
